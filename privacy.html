Privacy Policy

Last updated:

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our website and software application.

Information we collect

  • Website: Basic analytics data (such as page views) may be collected by our hosting provider.

  • Support: If you contact support, we receive the information you send (name, email, message).

How we use information

  • To provide and improve the product and customer support
  • To process licensing and deliver digital downloads
  • To prevent abuse, fraud, and unauthorized access

Data sharing

We share information only with service providers needed to operate the product (such as hosting and payments), or when required by law.

Data retention

We retain support and purchase records as needed for business operations, compliance, and troubleshooting.

Your choices

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your support data by contacting us at the email listed on the website.

Contact
